What We Offer

Culture change that actually changes how people think.

Most organizational training delivers content, frameworks, skills, protocols. Our consulting engagements go deeper. We work at the level of cognition itself: how your people perceive problems, make meaning, and collaborate across difference.

Drawing on Feuerstein's theory of Structural Cognitive Modifiability and Lonergan's philosophy of intentional consciousness, we design engagements that shift organizational culture from the inside out, through carefully mediated experiences that alter how staff think together, not just what they agree to do together.

We do not offer off-the-shelf training events. Every engagement is custom-designed with and for your institution, grounded in an honest assessment of where your organization is and where it needs to go.

"Organizations don't change because people learn new information. They change because people learn to think together differently."

Who It's For

For institutions ready to think differently.

Schools & Educational Institutions

Schools seeking to build genuine learning cultures, where staff and students alike are developing as thinkers, not merely performing tasks or meeting benchmarks.

Healthcare Organizations

Clinical teams and healthcare institutions working to improve how practitioners think through complexity, collaborate under pressure, and maintain reflective capacity in demanding environments.

Professional Associations & Firms

Professional associations and firms navigating organizational change, leadership development, or the cultivation of a reflective, adaptive culture across a distributed workforce.

Our Process

How a consulting engagement unfolds.

Every engagement is different, but all follow a common arc: honest assessment, careful design, facilitated experience, and meaningful follow-through.

1
Discovery & Assessment

We begin by listening, to leadership, staff, and where relevant, clients or students. We seek to understand the real cognitive and cultural dynamics at play, not just the presenting problem. This phase shapes everything that follows.

2
Custom Program Design

Drawing on the discovery phase, we co-design an engagement tailored to your institution's needs, culture, and goals. We do not adapt a standard program, we build yours from the ground up, with explicit attention to what will and won't work in your context.

3
Facilitated Workshops

We facilitate sessions designed as genuine mediated learning experiences, not presentations or trainings, but carefully orchestrated encounters that shift how participants see and think. Sessions may be with leadership, staff cohorts, or cross-functional groups.

4
Evaluation & Follow-Up

Real change takes time. We build in structured evaluation and follow-up, including optional ongoing consultation, to help your institution integrate and sustain what was learned. We are invested in outcomes, not just delivery.

Sample Engagements

What this looks like in practice.

Engagement A

Multi-Session Staff Development

A semester-long series of facilitated sessions with a school staff, building a shared vocabulary for cognitive development, examining real instructional challenges through a mediated learning lens, and developing internal capacity for ongoing reflective practice.

  • ✓ 6–8 facilitated sessions
  • ✓ Embedded in existing PD calendar
  • ✓ Leadership debrief & follow-up plan
Engagement B

Executive Team Reflection Retreat

A 2-day residential retreat for a senior leadership team, creating the conditions for genuine reflective inquiry into the organization's cognitive culture, shared assumptions, and capacity for adaptive change. Grounded in Lonergan's account of authentic subjectivity and collaborative decision-making.

  • ✓ 2-day residential format
  • ✓ Pre-retreat discovery interviews
  • ✓ Written synthesis & action framework
