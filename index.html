Core Programs
Our Foundation

Rooted in the science of how minds grow.

Founded on the landmark work of Lonergan and Feuerstein, the Insight Center bridges theory and practice in the service of human development. We believe that cognitive capacity is not fixed — it is modifiable, trainable, and expandable at every stage of life.

Our work is grounded in the conviction that insight — genuine, personal, and transformative — is the engine of lasting change.

What We Offer

Three paths to transformation.

Each of our programs is designed to develop the cognitive and emotional capacities that enable people to think more clearly and live more fully.

Cognitive Therapy

Individual and group therapy grounded in cognitive-mediational principles. We work at the level of thinking itself — helping clients develop the awareness and tools to reshape their own cognitive patterns.

Learn more

Therapist Training

Structured professional development for therapists and educators who want to integrate cognitive mediation into their practice. Training is experiential, theoretically grounded, and practically oriented.

Learn more

Consulting

Organizational and institutional consulting for schools, healthcare settings, and professional organizations seeking to build cultures of reflective practice and cognitive development at a systemic level.
