What We Offer

Building Stronger Learners

Many schools today face a common challenge: classrooms include students with a wide range of learning profiles, yet teachers are often given limited tools to address underlying cognitive development.

The Insight Center works with schools to strengthen the cognitive foundations of thinking and learning by introducing educators to philosopher Bernard Lonergan's formula for insightful reasoning and psychologist Reuven Feuerstein's principles of mediated learning.

This approach equips teachers to guide students in developing the thinking processes that support attention, reasoning, problem-solving, and independent learning.

Rather than focusing only on academic outcomes, The Insight Center aims to strengthen the mental operations that improve thinking and learning across all domains of life.

Our Services

What a consulting engagement entails.

Our consulting services typically include:

Faculty Training in Mediated Learning

We train teachers to incorporate mediated learning principles into everyday instruction so that cognitive development becomes part of the classroom culture.

Classroom Integration

We help schools integrate cognitive development practices across subjects and grade levels, enabling students to become more reflective, independent learners.

Options and Inclusion Programs

For schools seeking to better serve students with intellectual or developmental differences, we provide guidance on establishing programs that combine structured support with meaningful inclusion in the school's academic, social, and extracurricular life.
